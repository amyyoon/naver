GOOD HONEST SPIRITS MADE BY
HAND & ELEGANTLY CRAFTED FROM THE HEART.

de Vine Wines & Spirits showcases locally sourced ingredients through an offering of craft whisky, gin, vodka, rum, vermouth, and wines made using traditional small batch techniques.

FROM VINE TO WINE,OUR AWARDS
SPEAK TO OUR PROCESS.

All our wines are pressed, fermented, aged and bottled at de Vine. We process
organically without adding preservatives or sugar and solely use Vancouver Island
grown grapes.

FAR DOWN THE GENERATIONAL LINE
LIE OUR BEST KEPT SECRETS.

We pride ourselves on implementing the long, loving, laborious techniques of hand-harvesting, small-batch botanical blending, on-site grain milling and mashing, slow spirit stripping, traditional fermentation and distillation methods, whole-botanical aromatization, in-house barrel ageing, natural filtration, hand-bottling and labeling, and sensory evaluationâ€¦ to name a few.

OUR PHILOSOPHY HAS ALWAYS BEEN,AND
WILL ALWAYS BE AT THE CORE OF OUR BRAND.

We like to do things the hard way to stay true to our focus on quality and supporting local. We grow our own fruit, barley, and botanicals for our spirits, and partner with local growers and malters.
